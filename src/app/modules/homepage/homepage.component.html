1. Dear Customers, please be informed that the paymentPortal@SGCourts™ will undergo a scheduled maintenance from 5 Mar 2023 10:30pm to 6 Mar 2023 2:30am. During the maintenance period, all online payment options will not be available. We apologise for any inconvenience caused.

2. [Scam Advisory] Beware of emails/calls/SMSes falsely claiming to be from SGCourts, requesting for your personal or financial information. SG Courts will never send you any automated SMS / emails / calls with links requesting for your personal or financial information. If you receive any email/call purporting to be from SGCourts requesting for your personal or financial information, you should verify the authenticity of such instructions by calling SGCourts at 1800 999 9999 or emailing report-scam@judiciary.gov.sg before you respond.

Thank you.
